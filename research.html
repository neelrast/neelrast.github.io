Research

I enjoy combining upcoming research and existing frameworks to design end-to-end Deep Learning pipelines with an ultimate goal of creating a more human-centric and result oriented multimodal system. My primary interests spans the areas of multimodal human-robotic interactions, behavior cloning, natural language processing and generation, time series, quantitative analysis, deep learning, computer vision, software architecture, and agile design methods.

Below are some of my published work done at Collins College of Professional Studies, St. John's University, under supervision of Dr. Fazel Keshtkar, SJU-NLP Group.
