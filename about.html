About



I'm a Machine Learning Engineer and Data Science Professional, specializing in Natural Language Processing/Generation, Deep Learning and Computer Vision. Adept in conducting research and development within Scaled Automated Machine Learning and Human Robotic Interaction Systems, I am highly fascinated by the use of automation to accomplish key industry tasks of creating a more human-centric and result oriented multimodal systems.

Currently, I am working within the Clinical Values Team at Radiology Partners, which is one of the largest physician-led and physician-owned radiology practices within the U.S. Radiology Partners is a multi-state hospital-based practice, serving more than 100 locations, with the infrastructure and capital to scale further.

As part of this team, I work alongside a magnificent team of laureates, data scientists, engineers and analysts, to learn more from the collected domain expertise of our radiologists, and collaborate on design, implementation, and validation of solution components using cutting-edge algorithms to analyze diverse data as a means to achieve clinical initiatives and support practice objectives.

I also contribute to modeling and tuning current models for better at-par assisted identification and analysis of rad dictated reports to better serve our patients, perform data exploration on collected-studies and reports and research innovative solutions to apply towards the team’s clinical objectives while discovering business narratives told by the data and communicate them to other teammates, managers, and radiologists.
