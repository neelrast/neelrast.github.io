ABOUT ME

Hi! my name's Neel, and I am an AI Engineer and full-stack software developer.

Since past few years, I have been immensely involved within research in Human Robotic interactions and User Experience Design.

As, a computer science student and design enthusiast, I am big believer in design thinking, and have been really intrigued with the intersection between User experience and Machine Learning.

Growing up, I always looked up to something I really liked doing since I was a kid, which for me has been, my drive to always wanting to innovate and building products. Even before I joined college and came to a realization that I have always had a love for art and design, I was also intrigued and passionate to learn software development and be in computing as well, so, I decided to pursue Computer Science as a major, Mathematics as a minor and Art as an elective, and since then I have been able to explore many opportunities on many grounds, where I have learned to fall in love with the problem, and not just technology.

I seek deep understanding of problems that affect millions of people so we can explore creative new approaches to help solve them. I envision to build computing products which could reach many-many users and makes an effort to solve a real problem for them.

I strongly believe in the idea that the world’s biggest problems can not be solved with conventional, incremental thinking. Pushing for radical change is essential in such case, as it forces us to free ourselves from existing assumptions and start over, helping us get to an answer that’s practically unrecognizable from the status quo.
